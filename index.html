Barbershop Harmony, Refined

Quintessence is a male a cappella quartet dedicated to the fine art of barbershop and other close harmony choral styles. Our name brings to mind that special moment when all the notes in a chord are sung just right and the harmonic resonance produces a fifth note or overtone, as well as the medieval alchemists who sought the rare "fifth element" that could turn crude metals into gold.
